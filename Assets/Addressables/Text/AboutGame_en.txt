MDPro3 (hereinafter referred to as "this game") is a free and open-source Yu-Gi-Oh! OCG simulator.
The assets used in this project are provided for personal learning and community sharing only and are not for commercial use.

TRANSLATION NOTES

These translations began as fan projects. Over time, I replaced many of them with official card translations from <link="https://www.db.yugioh-card.com/yugiohdb/?request_locale=en/"><#0080F8><u>Yu-Gi-Oh! DB</u></color></link> and Master Duel wherever official releases existed.

For all supported languages except Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese, cards without an official TCG or OCG release still use fan translations. They will be updated with official text once an official release becomes available.

From 2024 to 2026, after we began collaborating with the developer SherryChaos and sharing MDPro3 through our server, the project grew significantly. We implemented many quality-of-life improvements, and there is still more to come.
